Food Delivery Mobile App

Client: Restaurant Collective
Timeline: 5 Months
Role: UX/UI Designer, Interaction Designer

Project Overview

A collective of local restaurants approached me to design a food delivery app that would allow them to compete with larger delivery platforms while maintaining direct relationships with their customers. The app needed to provide a seamless ordering experience while highlighting the unique offerings of each restaurant.

The client wanted a solution that would reduce their dependency on third-party delivery services, which were charging high commission fees and limiting their ability to provide personalized customer experiences.

The Challenge

The main challenges for this project included:

  • Creating a unified platform that could showcase diverse restaurant offerings
  • Designing an intuitive ordering system with customization options
  • Implementing real-time order tracking and notifications
  • Building a loyalty program that would work across multiple restaurants
  • Competing with established delivery apps in terms of user experience

The Process

01

User Research & Competitive Analysis

I conducted extensive user research to understand customer behaviors and preferences when ordering food online. This included interviews with regular food delivery app users, surveys, and competitive analysis of existing delivery platforms to identify opportunities for differentiation.

02

User Flows & Wireframing

I mapped out comprehensive user flows for key scenarios like browsing restaurants, placing orders, customizing items, and tracking deliveries. These flows informed the creation of low and medium-fidelity wireframes that were tested with potential users to validate the core functionality and navigation.

03

Visual Design & Branding

I developed a vibrant, food-centric visual design system that would appeal to hungry users while maintaining clarity and usability. The design included custom illustrations, food photography guidelines, and a color palette that evoked appetite and excitement while ensuring accessibility.

04

Prototyping & User Testing

I created high-fidelity interactive prototypes that simulated the complete ordering experience. These prototypes were tested with a diverse group of users in realistic scenarios, providing valuable feedback that informed several iterations and refinements to the design.

The Solution

The final food delivery app featured:

  • A personalized home screen highlighting favorite restaurants and dishes
  • Advanced filtering options for dietary preferences and cuisine types
  • Intuitive menu browsing with detailed item customization
  • Real-time order tracking with map visualization and status updates
  • A unified loyalty program with points and rewards across all restaurants
  • In-app messaging for direct communication with restaurants

The food delivery app has been successfully adopted by both restaurants and customers. Within the first six months after launch, participating restaurants reported a 32% increase in order volume compared to their previous delivery methods.

The app maintains an impressive 85% user retention rate and has received positive reviews in app stores, with an average rating of 4.7/5. Users particularly appreciate the real-time tracking, customization options, and the unified loyalty program.

Conclusion

This project demonstrates the power of user-centered design in creating digital products that serve multiple stakeholders. By focusing on the needs of both restaurants and customers, we were able to create a food delivery platform that provides a superior experience to users while helping local restaurants maintain direct relationships with their customers and reduce dependency on third-party services.
